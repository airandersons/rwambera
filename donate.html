Your beat matters

Every donation fuels the heartbeat of our culture.

Contributions help us:

  • Cultural performances – bring rhythm to weddings, festivals and ceremonies
  • Drum equipment – repair and build traditional drums (djembe, engalabi, entamiiwu)
  • Training young performers – free drum lessons for Busabala youth
  • Community cultural events – organize celebrations that unite generations
  • Preserving African drumming traditions – document and teach ancestral rhythms
