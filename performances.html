More than music – a living experience

Every Rwambera performance is a fusion of raw energy, precise choreography, and ancient rhythms.

Electric energy – polyrhythms that make crowds move involuntarily
Choreographed movement – synchronized drummers, dancers and fire performers
Traditional instruments – djembe, engalabi, entamiiwu, and modern bass drums
Theatrical storytelling – each rhythm tells a story of ancestors, harvest or celebration

Whether it's a 10-minute curtain-raiser or a full-hour spectacle, we tailor the set to your audience. Expect call-and-response, audience participation, and a finale that brings everyone to their feet.
